#+TITLE: about

lameserver.net is one person's board: philes about unix, emacs, nix, and
whatever else survives the draft folder. Written in org-mode, built by a
single elisp file, styled after the ANSI scene, signed with GPG.

- the sysop goes by *grim* · =grim@samhain.su=
- every phile ships its org source and a detached signature
- the public key lives at [[https://lameserver.net/pubkey.asc][/pubkey.asc]]
- the whole site is one repo: [[https://github.com/theesfeld/lame-server][github.com/theesfeld/lame-server]]

No comments, no analytics, no javascript required to read. If you want to
reply to a phile, write something better on your own board and sign it.
